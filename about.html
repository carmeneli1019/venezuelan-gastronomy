Marquesa de Chocolate

Ingredients

Method

  • 2 packages of Maria Cookies
  • Chocolate chips
  • 1 liter of milk
  • 1 cup of milk (to dip the cookies)
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 300 grams of black chocolate for desserts
  • 2 butter spoons
  • 120 grams of sugar (optional)
  • Vanilla
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • A bit of liquor for the chocolate, rum, for example (optional)

In a heavy-bottomed pot place the milk, sugar and vanilla, when it is warm add the previously beaten egg yolks, stir constantly, do not stop stirring since the mixture must be homogeneous and the egg must be prevented from cooking for pieces and do not mix well.

In the meantime, in a bain-marie melt the chocolate with the butter, stirring and stirring the milk mixture, when it is melted, pour it into the milk and continue stirring, dissolve the cornstarch in a little milk (it can be in the blender) and add it to the previous preparation, if you want you can add a splash of liquor, continue stirring until it thickens, this can last several minutes, the consistency should not be too thick since when it cools it is thick a little more. Lower the heat and reserve.

To put everything together, take a package of Maria cookies, and in a glass mold place first a layer of cookies previously soaked in milk, then a layer of pudding, continue layering until finishing with chocolate pudding, you can decorate with Chocolate rain or cookie pieces, there are also those who alternate sliced ​​and toasted almonds or chocolate chips with pudding and cookies, others dip the cookies in liquor, like most of our recipes, each one adds a personal and creative touch.
